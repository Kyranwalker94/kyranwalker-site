🍅 Pico de Gallo

The classic fresh Mexican salsa. Bright, zingy and dead simple, tomato, onion, lime and coriander at their best.

🍅
🕐 10 mins 🍽️ Serves 4-6 🌶️ Mild-Hot 🌱 Vegan

Ingredients

  • 4 medium ripe tomatoes
  • ½ white onion
  • 1-2 jalapeños (adjust to taste)
  • Large bunch of fresh coriander
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tsp salt (plus more to taste)

Method

  1. Dice the tomatoes into small, even pieces, remove the seeds and excess liquid for a less watery salsa.
  2. Finely chop the onion as small as you can get it.
  3. Halve the jalapeños, remove seeds for milder heat, then finely chop.
  4. Roughly chop the coriander leaves (stems are fine too).
  5. Combine tomato, onion and jalapeño in a bowl.
  6. Add lime juice and salt. Stir well.
  7. Fold in the coriander.
  8. Taste and adjust, more lime for brightness, more salt to bring out the tomato.
  9. Leave to sit for 15 minutes before serving so the flavours meld.
💡 Armijos Tip Pat your diced tomatoes dry on kitchen paper before mixing, it keeps the pico fresh and scoopable rather than watery. Best eaten the same day.
