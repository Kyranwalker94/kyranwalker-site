🌿 Chimichurri

Punchy Argentinian herb sauce. Incredible on steak, great on chicken, surprisingly good on everything else too.

🌿
🕐 15 mins + rest 🍽️ Makes 1 jar 🌶️ Herby · Medium 🌱 Vegan

Ingredients

  • Large bunch of flat-leaf parsley (about 60g)
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes (add more for heat)
  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 100ml good olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Optional: small handful of fresh oregano or coriander

Method

  1. Finely chop the parsley, leaves and tender stems. Do this by hand for the best texture; a food processor makes it too fine and wet.
  2. Peel and mince the garlic as fine as you can get it.
  3. Combine parsley, garlic, dried oregano and chilli flakes in a bowl.
  4. Pour in the red wine vinegar and stir.
  5. Add the olive oil and mix well, the sauce should be loose and spoonable, not a paste.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Taste and adjust the vinegar (tang) or chilli (heat).
  7. Leave to rest for at least 30 minutes before serving, the flavours deepen significantly. Overnight is even better.
💡 Armijos Tip Chimichurri keeps well in a jar in the fridge for up to a week. The oil will solidify, just bring it back to room temperature before serving and give it a stir.
