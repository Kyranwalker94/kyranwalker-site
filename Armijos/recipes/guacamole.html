🥑 Guacamole

Proper fresh guac, creamy, limey, no nonsense. No sour cream, no mayo. Just avocado done right.

🥑
🕐 10 mins 🍽️ Serves 4 🌶️ Mild 🌱 Vegan

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • Juice of 1½ limes
  • 1 small clove of garlic, finely minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Pinch of cumin (optional but recommended)
  • Small handful of fresh coriander, chopped
  • Optional: ¼ white onion, finely diced
  • Optional: ½ jalapeño, finely chopped

Method

  1. Halve the avocados, remove the stones, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Add the lime juice straight away to prevent browning.
  3. Mash with a fork, keep it chunky. Guac should have texture, not be a smooth paste.
  4. Add the minced garlic, salt and cumin. Mix well.
  5. If using onion and jalapeño, fold them in now.
  6. Stir in the chopped coriander.
  7. Taste and adjust, more lime for brightness, more salt if it tastes flat.
  8. Serve immediately, or press cling film directly onto the surface to stop it going brown.
💡 Armijos Tip The avocado must be properly ripe, it should yield to gentle pressure but not be mushy. An underripe avocado will taste grassy and waxy no matter what you add to it.
