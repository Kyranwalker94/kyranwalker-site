🍍 Pineapple Salsa

Sweet tropical heat. Fresh pineapple and red chilli make this one the crowd-pleaser — brilliant with tacos, grilled chicken or just a bag of tortilla chips.

🍍
🕐 15 mins 🍽️ Serves 4–6 🌶️ Sweet · Medium 🌱 Vegan

Ingredients

  • ½ fresh pineapple (about 300g), peeled and cored
  • ½ red onion
  • 1 red chilli (or jalapeño for milder heat)
  • Small bunch of fresh coriander
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: pinch of cumin for depth

Method

  1. Cut the pineapple into small, even cubes — about 1cm. You want bite-sized pieces, not a mush.
  2. Finely dice the red onion.
  3. Remove chilli seeds for less heat, then finely chop.
  4. Roughly chop the coriander.
  5. Combine pineapple, onion and chilli in a bowl.
  6. Squeeze over the lime juice and add a pinch of salt.
  7. Fold in the coriander (and cumin if using).
  8. Mix gently and taste — it should be bright, sweet and punchy.
  9. Refrigerate for 20 minutes before serving for best flavour.
💡 Armijos Tip Use fresh pineapple, not tinned — tinned is too soft and watery. If the pineapple is very sweet, add a tiny extra squeeze of lime to balance it out.
