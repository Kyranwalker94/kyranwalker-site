Game Scratch CS50

Pokémon Snap ⚡

A 2-player reaction game built in Scratch for CS50, match your Pokémon, hit your number first, first to 10 wins.

About the project

Built for the first week of CS50, this is a 2-player reaction game played on a single keyboard. Both players take turns sending out a Pokémon, when both players' Pokémon match, a number appears on screen. The first player to press their number wins the round and their Pokémon plays its sound from the show. First to 10 wins.

Play it

Click the green flag to start. Best played with two people on one keyboard.
