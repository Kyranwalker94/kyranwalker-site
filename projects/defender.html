About the project

In September 2023 I bought a 2008 Land Rover Defender 110. It was in okay condition but definitely needed work. The plan is to fully restore the truck — every part replaced so it'll last — and build it out as a camper and expedition vehicle, with the long-term goal of an extended overland trip.
