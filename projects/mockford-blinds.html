Mockford Blinds 🪟

Designed and built a website, ran digital ads, and helped run the family business, generating over £70,000 in sales.

* Mockford Blinds is no longer operating.

About the project

Mockford Blinds was a family business supplying handmade interior blinds and awnings to the Eastbourne and Seaford area, run by my grandparents.

In early 2020 they asked me to design and build a website for the business and help with other areas. I designed and built mockfordblinds.co.uk from scratch and set up Google and Facebook ad campaigns to drive traffic. The enquiries started coming in almost immediately.

Going deeper

Due to the surge in business, and the challenges the pandemic brought, my grandparents asked me to support the business more directly for a year. I agreed and got stuck in across all areas: updating Xero to maintain the books, learning to manufacture vertical blinds, fitting various blind and awning types on-site, and handling customer relations.

The star product was exterior awnings. I focused the advertising on these and used 3D modelling to create photorealistic mockups showing clients exactly what an awning would look like installed on their property, a powerful sales tool.

In the first 9 months, the business made over £70,000 in sales.

What I did

  • Designed and built the company website from scratch
  • Set up and managed Google Ads and Facebook Ads campaigns
  • Created 3D awning mockups to use as a sales tool
  • Maintained accounts and bookkeeping in Xero
  • Learned to manufacture vertical blinds
  • Fitted blinds and awnings on-site across the Eastbourne and Seaford area
  • Generated over £70,000 in sales in the first 9 months
