Lockdown Quiz 🎯

A family quiz game built in Scratch during lockdown — with specialist categories, score tracking, and Twitch streaming support.

About the project

When lockdown started I wanted to keep the family connected and having fun, so I built this quiz in Scratch. A quizmaster's assistant streams the quiz live on Twitch — questions go out via WhatsApp, players answer, and results are entered before the next question is released. The game tallies scores automatically.

There are 6 categories and players pick a specialist category before the game starts. Correct answers in specialist categories earn a bonus 5 points — but a wrong answer costs 5.
