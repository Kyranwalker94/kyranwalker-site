General Knowledge Quiz 🧠

An endless multiple-choice quiz — answer as many questions as you can before your streak breaks. Top streaks go on the leaderboard.

🧠

Game in development

The quiz is being built. It'll pull from thousands of questions across categories, track your streak, and let you put your name on the leaderboard.

How it works

Questions are pulled live from the Open Trivia Database — a community-maintained bank of thousands of general knowledge questions across dozens of categories. No two sessions are the same.

  • Pick a category and difficulty, or play mixed for maximum variety
  • Four answer options per question — one correct
  • Each correct answer extends your streak
  • Wrong answer ends the game — your streak is your score
  • Qualify for the leaderboard with a top-10 streak — enter a 3-letter name
  • Leaderboard is shared and persistent — beat the current top score
